WhiteTree — anti-raid & server protection

Getting started

WhiteTree protects Discord servers against raids, nukes, spam and fake accounts. All protections are on by default — you can invite the bot and it works immediately, no setup required.

On join, WhiteTree automatically creates a Quarantine role. Make sure the bot's role is placed high in your role list so it can act on members.

Invite the bot

Use the invite link with the recommended permissions (manage roles & channels, kick, ban, timeout, view audit log). Then move the WhiteTree role near the top of Server Settings → Roles.

Protections

Anti-raid — detects join waves (X joins in Y seconds) and locks the server, raising Discord's native verification level and applying slowmode, restoring everything after.

Anti-nuke — watches the audit log for mass channel/role deletions, webhook creation, mass bans/kicks and permission escalation; the responsible account is quarantined.

Anti-spam — flood, repeated messages, excessive mentions, @everyone and unauthorized discord.gg invites are deleted and the author is timed out.

New accounts — a risk score (account age, avatar, bot-like name, spammer flag) filters suspicious accounts at the gate.

Anti-impersonation — flags usernames that copy your staff, including accents and full-width unicode.

Verification panel

Run /verifypanel in a channel to post a “Verify” button. New members must click it to gain access — raid bots don't click, so it's a very effective gate. Passing members get their roles back automatically.

Dashboard

The dashboard shows each server's live protection status, recent sanctions and quarantined members, and lets you change the bot's language. Sign in with Discord to see the servers where you're an admin and WhiteTree is installed.

The dashboard runs alongside the bot. On a home PC it lives at localhost:8080; with a tunnel or host it becomes a real public link.

FAQ

Do I need to configure anything?

No. Protections are active on invite. Optionally run /setup #logs to see alerts.

The bot can't sanction members

Move the WhiteTree role higher than the roles it should manage, and keep the listed permissions.

How do I keep it running 24/7?

Host it on a small server (Oracle Cloud free tier, a VPS, or a device that stays on).
